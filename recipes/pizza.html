Maragherita Pizza

This simple yet elegant pizza is both quick to bake and easy to enjoy

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Marinara Sauce
  • mozeralla cheese
  • tomatoes
  • basil

Steps

  1. cover crust with marinara sauce
  2. sprinkle cheese over the top of sauce
  3. sprikle basil leaves over cheese
  4. cook in oven on 400 degrees for 20 minutes
