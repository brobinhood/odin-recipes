Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna

This is possibly the best lasagna recipe in the world. I hope you enjoy trying it.

Ingredients

  • marinara sauce
  • lasagna pasta noodles
  • mozeralla cheese

Steps

  1. place noodles in 9x13 inch glass pan
  2. pour sauce over noodles
    3. spinkle cheese on top
  3. cook in oven on 400 degrees for 45 minutes
